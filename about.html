About Us - The Colibri Foundation

Envisioning a World Healed by Art

Our Vision

In a world where mental health challenges are increasingly prevalent, the Colibri Foundation envisions a society where art is universally recognized as a vital tool for healing, expression, and connection. We imagine a future where every individual, regardless of background or circumstance, has access to the transformative power of art therapy, fostering a global community that values creativity, empathy, and psychological well-being.

Our Mission

The mission of the Colibri Foundation is to promote mental health and emotional well-being through the power of art therapy. We are dedicated to creating safe, inclusive, and nurturing spaces where individuals can explore, heal, and grow through artistic expression. Our multifaceted approach includes providing art therapy workshops, collaborating with educational institutions, engaging in community art projects, and advocating for the integration of art therapy in mental health practices.

Core Values
  1. Creativity as a Catalyst for Healing: We believe in the inherent healing power of creativity and its ability to transform lives.
  2. Inclusivity and Diversity: Our commitment to inclusivity is unwavering, embracing individuals from all walks of life, acknowledging the richness that diversity brings to our community.
  3. Empathy and Compassion: At the heart of our work lies deep empathy and compassion, guiding our interactions and shaping our approach to healing.
  4. Innovation and Adaptability: We strive to be at the forefront of art therapy, continuously adapting and innovating to meet the evolving needs of those we serve.
  5. Collaboration and Community: Building strong, collaborative relationships within our community is key to amplifying our impact and creating lasting change.
