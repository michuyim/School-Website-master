Colibri Foundation
Wings of Hope

The Colibri Foundation, since its inception in 2005, has been a beacon of hope and creativity. Born from a shared vision to harness the transformative power of art, our journey reflects a commitment to mental health and community well-being.

Humble
Beginnings

In the early years, our founders, a diverse group of artists, therapists, and community activists, recognized the potential of art as a tool for healing and social change. The foundation began with small, local workshops focusing on art therapy for children and adults facing mental health challenges.

Our
Team

At the Colibri Foundation, our team is our greatest asset. Comprised of passionate individuals from various backgrounds, each member brings a unique set of skills and experiences, united by a common goal: to make a difference through art.

Celebrate our 1st anniversary with a large-scale art exhibition showcasing the works of our workshop participants.
